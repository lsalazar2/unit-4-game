Crystals Collector Game

Instructions

You will be given a random number at the start of the game. There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific number of points to your total score. The value of the crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number shown on the screen. You lose the game by going above that number.

Each time a new game is started a new random number will be shown and each crystal will get a new value. HAVE FUN!!!
